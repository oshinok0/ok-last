Silay City

Ohh, Silay City, what’s not to love? This is the place to be if you enjoy rich culture and antique treasures. In reality, the city is overflowing with well-maintained Spanish-style colonial homes, delectable regional cuisine, and breathtaking scenery.

Bacolod City

Bacolod is one of the most forward-thinking cities in the Philippines. It is an urban area in Negros Occidental that takes pride in its eclectic mix of historical, cultural, and religious sites. Bacolod is known as the City of Smiles thanks to the Masskara Festival and the friendly residents.
